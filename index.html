About Grandma Shoshana

Grandma Shoshana was born in Poland in 1937 to her parents Perl and Yitzhak. During World War II, the family moved to Russia, and later made their way to Israel to build a new life. In Israel, Shoshana met her beloved husband Yosef. Together, they settled in Holon and raised six sons. Over the years, their family grew to include many grandchildren and great-grandchildren.
